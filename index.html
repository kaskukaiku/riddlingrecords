Intro

Riddling Records started out just as a way to get our music out there, for whomever it may be meant to. Turns out, we are not alone in this endeavor - we are making waves. Some work of ours has been noticed on major playlists, and hey, we're just getting started.

As a company run by us artists ourselves, dividing the pie in a just way is a key value for us. Our producers get their fair share, and so do engineers, visual artists, and all creators involved. Our livelihood doesn't depend on the music, which allows authenticity and purity in the work. We have been attracting soulful artists, to us music comes first in the music business. If you're an artist looking for your home, shoot us a DM.

If you're a lover of music as an art, with a deeper meaning, be sure to test some of our songs out. Right now new material is coming from both Violetti Aleksi and Kasku, and if you're a rapper or a singer-songwriter - Meraki is always working. Check out more on the Artists tab.

Artists

Violetti Aleksi

Violetti Aleksi - also known as @violettialeksi - combines a flow of a youngen with the wisdom of an OG. Violetti Aleksi's debut EP 'Violetti Planeetta' will be released in early 2025, with many more tracks on the way.

Meraki

Meraki, also known as @merakigotbeats - is the powerhouse behind much of Riddling Records' production. As a gifted beatmaker and a talented sound engineer, he is known to deliver top-notch sound at the drop of a dime. Be sure to follow him on IG and if you need a sound engineer, he is your man. DM is your key to success.

Natalie Laine

Natalie - also known as @han_on_perhonen - is Riddling Records' visual genius behind much of the artwork on our releases. Natalie is a master in doing physical art and has a certain pride in her craft, accompanied by a sensitive radar for emotion. Be sure to follow her on IG, you will be blown away. You might wanna ask her to make portrait of you (or your loved one)!. DM for the win.

Kasku

Kasku - also known as @kaskuraps - is best known for his mind-blowing freestyles and recently taking that skill to the next level with written verses. Kasku's specialty is deep and soulful lyrics combined with eccentric, one-of-one pockets in his flow.
